  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  • In each turn, a player rolls the dice as many times as he whishes. Each result gets added to his ROUND score
  • BUT, if the player rolls a 1, or a 6 twice in a row, all his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • The first player to reach 100 points or a chosen Final Score on their GLOBAL score wins the game
